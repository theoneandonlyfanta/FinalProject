Fastest Volkswagen Cars

Volkswagen Golf R

Among hatchbacks, the Volkswagen Golf R is a remarkable combination of performance and usability. The 2.0-liter four-cylinder turbocharged engine in the Golf R produces about 315 horsepower and 310 lb-ft of torque. The Golf R's powerful engine and 4MOTION all-wheel drive technology enable it to go from 0 to 60 miles per hour in just 4.5 to 4.7 seconds, demonstrating its exceptional speed and acceleration capabilities. The car maintains impressive agility on both twisting highways and city streets thanks to its accurate steering and nimble handling. In addition to its performance, the Golf R's interior is both cozy and useful, which makes it a desirable option for enthusiasts looking for a little car that can do daily tasks and go fast.

Volkswagen Arteon R

In the fastback class, the Volkswagen Arteon R is a striking combination of luxury and powerful performance. The 2.0-liter four-cylinder turbocharged engine of the Arteon R generates about 316 horsepower and 310 lb-ft of torque. The Arteon R's 4MOTION all-wheel-drive system effectively channels this powerful engine, allowing it to accelerate from 0 to 60 miles per hour in an only 5.0 seconds, demonstrating its remarkable speed and acceleration capabilities. A balanced driving experience is provided by the car's deft handling, responsive suspension, and precise steering, which guarantee both dynamic performance and comfort. Beyond its performance qualities, the Arteon R boasts a refined and expertly designed interior that perfectly combines luxury and sportiness. It is furnished with cutting-edge technology, contemporary conveniences, and premium materials.

Volkswagen ID.4 GTX

The Volkswagen ID.4 GTX is the company's entry into the electric SUV market, fusing efficiency with cutting-edge technology and remarkable performance. The ID.4 GTX's dual-motor arrangement provides 339 lb-ft of quick electric torque and about 295 horsepower to all four wheels. The ID.4 GTX exhibits fair acceleration and rapid response, reaching 0 to 60 mph in about 6.2 seconds, despite its electric drivetrain and focus on sustainability. A stable and enjoyable driving experience is provided by the SUV's agile and quick handling, which is enhanced by the all-wheel-drive system, particularly on varied road conditions. Beyond its performance stats, the ID.4 GTX has a roomy and technologically advanced interior that includes driver-assistance capabilities, state-of-the-art infotainment systems, and a cozy cabin fit for daily use.
