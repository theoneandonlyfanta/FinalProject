Mercedes-Benz's Fastest Cars

Mercedes-AMG GT

The Mercedes-AMG GT is the highest performing and most luxurious model in the Mercedes-Benz range. With a custom-built AMG 4.0-liter V8 biturbo engine, the AMG GT can produce an amazing 469 to 577 horsepower, depending on the model and trim level. The AMG GT's exceptional acceleration and speed capabilities are demonstrated by the speed at which it can reach 60 mph in just 3.5 to 3.9 seconds when paired with its potent engine and quick-shifting dual-clutch transmission. It offers outstanding control and reactivity thanks to its deft handling and accurate steering, making driving on the track and on open highways an exciting experience. The AMG GT's aerodynamic and streamlined exterior accentuates its dynamic attributes, while Mercedes-Benz's opulent interior demonstrates the brand's dedication to style and cutting-edge technology.

Mercedes-AMG C63

Among tiny luxury sedans, the Mercedes-AMG C63 is the pinnacle of performance and luxury combined. A strong 4.0-liter V8 biturbo engine that is handcrafted and produces an amazing 469 to 503 horsepower, depending on the model and variation, powers the AMG C63. The AMG C63's powerful engine and quick-shifting gearbox enable it to reach 60 mph in a little 3.8 to 4.0 seconds, demonstrating its remarkable acceleration and top speed. The AMG C63 provides accurate handling and confident maneuvering together with a dynamic and captivating driving experience that goes beyond its performance. Its rich interior, which is furnished with premium materials and cutting-edge technology, along with its elegant and stylish exterior, represent Mercedes-Benz's dedication to performance and luxury.

Mercedes-AMG S65

Within the Mercedes-Benz portfolio, the Mercedes-AMG S65 is the pinnacle of performance and luxury. This luxurious sedan's customized 6.0-liter V12 biturbo engine produces an incredible 621 horsepower and a massive 738 lb-ft of torque when driven. The AMG S65's enormous powertrain and quick, multi-speed automatic transmission enable it to accelerate from 0 to 60 miles per hour in about 4.0 seconds, demonstrating its amazing acceleration in spite of its greater size. The driving experience of the S65 is distinguished by its elegant and collected manner, providing a well-balanced combination of comfort and power. The vehicle's luxury interior highlights its commitment to provide an exceptional and refined driving experience, with premium materials, cutting-edge technology, and extravagant amenities.
