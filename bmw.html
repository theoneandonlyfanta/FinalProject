BMW's Fastest Cars

BMW M5

Power, luxury, and state-of-the-art technology are all expertly combined in the legendary BMW M5, a high-performance sedan. With a powerful 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine, the M5 can generate an amazing 617 to 600 horsepower, depending on the model and trim level. The M5 can reach 60 mph in a remarkable 3.1 to 3.4 seconds because to its powerful engine, sophisticated all-wheel-drive system, and precise eight-speed automatic transmission. This impressive acceleration highlights the car's elite speed potential. Outstanding agility and handling make driving the M5 a balanced and exhilarating experience on the road as well as the racetrack. Its quick steering and active suspension provide exact control and performance, displaying its sports car DNA in a sophisticated sedan.

BMW M8

Within the brand's portfolio, the BMW M8 is the pinnacle of performance, power, and luxury. Fuelled by a powerful 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine, the M8 produces a remarkable 617 to 600 horsepower, depending on the model and trim level. With its sophisticated all-wheel-drive system and accurate eight-speed automatic transmission, this powerful engine allows the M8 to go from 0 to 60 miles per hour in an incredible 3.0 to 3.2 seconds, demonstrating its exceptional acceleration and top-tier speed capabilities. The M8 offers a smooth and thrilling driving experience on the open road as well as on the racetrack thanks to its remarkable handling and agility.

BMW i8

The BMW i8 is a unique plug-in hybrid sports automobile that combines cutting-edge styling, environmentally friendly technology, and potent performance. With its three-cylinder gasoline engine and electric motor working together, the i8 produces about 369 horsepower total power. The i8's sophisticated all-wheel-drive system and hybrid drivetrain enable it to go from 0 to 60 miles per hour in a mere 4.2 to 4.4 seconds, demonstrating its remarkable efficiency and performance. A exciting yet environmentally responsible ride is guaranteed by the i8's driving experience, which combines sporty handling and environmental awareness. Its agility is enhanced by its lightweight design and well adjusted suspension, which makes it fun to drive on both winding and metropolitan streets.
